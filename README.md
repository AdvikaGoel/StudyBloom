# 🌸 Study Bloom - Making Study Easy

**Your personal study companion - designed for students who want to learn better!**

## ✨ Features

✅ **Create & Manage Notes** - Organize your study materials beautifully  
✅ **Track Progress** - Monitor your learning journey  
✅ **Offline Support** - Study anywhere, anytime  
✅ **Beautiful UI** - Pastel design that's easy on the eyes  
✅ **Mobile Ready** - Works on any device  
✅ **Fast & Lightweight** - No lag, just smooth studying  

## 🚀 Quick Start

### Option 1: Use Online (Recommended)
👉 **[Open Study Bloom](https://advikagoel.github.io/StudyBloom/)**

### Option 2: Install as App
1. Open the above link on your phone
2. Tap the menu (⋮) → "Install app" (Android) or Share → "Add to Home Screen" (iOS)
3. Your app is ready!

### Option 3: Download from Google Play Store
👉 **[Download on Google Play](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.advikagoel.studybloom)**

## 📝 How to Use

1. **Add a Note** - Click "Add Note" and start typing
2. **Edit Notes** - Click on any note to edit or update
3. **Delete Notes** - Swipe or click delete to remove
4. **View Progress** - See your study stats at the top
5. **Export Data** - Save your notes (feature coming soon)

## 💻 Tech Stack

- **Frontend:** HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript
- **Storage:** Local Storage (offline support)
- **Deployment:** GitHub Pages, Google Play Store
- **Build:** Cordova (for Android APK)
